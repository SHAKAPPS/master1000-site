# Privacy Policy — Master 1000
Last updated: July 12, 2026

Master 1000 ("we", "us") operates the Master 1000 mobile application (the "App"). This policy explains what we collect, why, and your rights. By using the App you agree to this policy.

## 1. Information We Collect
**Account information.** Name and email address when you create an account (directly or via Google/Apple sign-in). Guest mode requires no account; guest progress is stored on your device.

**Learning data.** Your language selections, lesson activity, answers, review schedule, scores, streaks, XP, certificates, and settings. We use this to run the Smart Recall scheduling system and sync your progress across devices.

**Speech data.** In Speak mode, your microphone captures short audio clips of you saying practice sentences. Clips are sent securely to our servers and processed by our speech-assessment providers (Microsoft Azure Speech Services and, for some languages, OpenAI transcription) to recognize what you said and score your pronunciation. Recordings are processed transiently and are not retained after assessment; only the recognized text, scores, and technical metadata are kept as part of your learning data. On unsupported devices, recognition may instead be performed by your device's operating system (Apple Speech framework) subject to Apple's terms.

**Purchase information.** Subscriptions are processed by Apple. We never see or store your payment card details. We receive purchase status (e.g., active/expired) via our subscription-management provider (RevenueCat) to unlock Premium features.

**Device & usage data.** App version, device model, OS version, language, crash logs, and anonymized usage analytics (screens visited, feature usage) to improve the App.

## 2. What We Do NOT Collect
We do not collect your contacts, photos, location, or advertising identifiers. We do not sell personal data. We do not show third-party ads.

## 3. How We Use Information
- Provide, personalize, and sync your learning experience
- Schedule reviews and compute forecasts from your performance
- Process subscriptions and unlock Premium
- Send optional practice reminders (you control these in Settings and in system notification settings)
- Fix bugs, prevent abuse, and improve the App

## 4. Legal Bases (EEA/UK users)
Performance of contract (providing the service), legitimate interests (analytics, security), and consent (notifications, microphone access — revocable at any time in device settings).

## 5. Data Sharing
We share data only with processors that operate the App on our behalf: cloud database and authentication (Supabase), subscription management (RevenueCat), speech assessment (Microsoft Azure Speech Services; OpenAI for transcription in some languages), and crash/analytics tooling. Each is bound by data-processing agreements. We may disclose data if required by law.

## 6. Data Retention & Deletion
Learning data is kept while your account is active. You may delete your account (and all associated data) at any time from Profile → Settings → Delete account, or by emailing us. Deletion is completed within 30 days. Guest data lives only on your device and is removed when you delete the App.

## 7. Your Rights
Depending on your location (GDPR, CCPA, Israeli Privacy Protection Law), you may request access, correction, export, or deletion of your data, and object to processing. Contact: privacy@master1000.app.

## 8. Children
The App is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect data from children. Contact us for removal if you believe a child provided data.

## 9. Security
Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. Access is limited by role. No method is 100% secure; report concerns to security@master1000.app.

## 10. International Transfers
Data may be processed in countries other than yours, with appropriate safeguards (e.g., SCCs).

## 11. Changes
We will post changes here and update the date above. Material changes will be announced in the App.
