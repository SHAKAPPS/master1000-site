# Paywall / App Store Subscription Disclosure

Use this text on or immediately next to the purchase button. The App Store product data returned by StoreKit must remain the source of truth for price and billing period.

## English

### Master 1000 Premium

**Monthly Premium** — $9.99 per month  
**Annual Premium** — $59.99 per year, including a 7-day free trial for eligible users  
**Lifetime Access** — $129.99 one-time purchase

Prices may vary by country and are shown in your local currency by the App Store. In Israel, the intended prices are ₪34.90 monthly, ₪199.90 annually, and ₪449.90 for Lifetime Access. The price displayed by Apple at checkout controls.

Payment will be charged to your Apple ID at confirmation of purchase. Monthly and annual subscriptions automatically renew unless auto-renewal is turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Your Apple ID may be charged for renewal within 24 hours before the current period ends.

The 7-day trial applies to eligible annual-plan users. Unless canceled at least 24 hours before the trial ends, it converts to the paid annual plan at the price shown at enrollment.

Manage or cancel at **Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions**. Deleting the App or your Master 1000 account does not cancel an Apple subscription. Use **Restore Purchases** to restore an eligible purchase.

Lifetime Access is a one-time, non-renewing purchase for the commercially supported lifetime of the Master 1000 service, subject to the Terms of Use.
