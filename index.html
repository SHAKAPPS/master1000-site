Master 1000

Learn to actually speak a new language.

Master 1000 is a language-learning app for iPhone and iPad. It teaches the 1,000 most useful everyday sentences in 23 languages, using spaced-repetition “Smart Recall” reviews and AI-scored speaking practice — so you learn to hold real conversations, not just memorize words. The first 100 sentences are free forever; a Premium subscription unlocks every level and language.
